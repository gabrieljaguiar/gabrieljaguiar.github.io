A comprehensive analysis of concept drift locality in data streams

This website provides interactive plots and tables for the experimental study on locality of concept drifts. The manuscript preprint is available at arXiv or ResearchGate and was submitted to the journal Pattern Recognition for publication.

The source code, algorithms, and experimental setup are provided on the Github repository. Results and benchmark data for all experiments are available to download as csv files to facilitate the reproducibility of the experimental study.
